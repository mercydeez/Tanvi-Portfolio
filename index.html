About Tanvi

Tanvi Janorkar is an Indian classical vocalist and television host. She keeps her Hindustani training at the center and brings a clear, calm presence on screen.

She is the writer, producer, editor, and anchor of the first Marathi special show for GenZs in her channel's history, shaping youth stories with a fresh voice.

Tanvi works with studios, channels, and podcast teams, balancing music depth with focused, friendly interviews.
