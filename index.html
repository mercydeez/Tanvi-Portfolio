About Tanvi

A versatile artist whose voice carries the timeless essence of Indian classical music, while her presence lights up television screens and live stages across the nation.

With years of rigorous training in Hindustani classical music and a natural flair for communication, Tanvi has carved a unique niche as a performer who connects deeply with her audience. Whether rendering a soulful raag or hosting a prestigious event, she brings the same dedication, warmth, and professionalism.

Her journey spans intimate mehfils, grand concert halls, corporate galas, and national television broadcasts. As an anchor and TV host, she has graced numerous award ceremonies, cultural festivals, and entertainment shows, earning acclaim for her eloquence, screen presence, and ability to engage diverse audiences.
